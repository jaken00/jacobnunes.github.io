About Me

My interests center on game engines, game development, and systems programming. I enjoy studying the systems and mechanisms that support large-scale online worlds. I'm especially interested in simulations, particularly the possibilities of using AI to generate history, narrative, and emergent behavior within virtual worlds.
I like working on projects where I can rebuild or reimplement classic engine techniques, study their tradeoffs, and understand how performant software is constructed from the ground up.

Languages I use: Python, C, Go, SQL

Projects

    Aethermoor is an attempt to create a persistant world simulation with entities (mobs, players, factions, empires) that follow a need based descsion tree that results in stores, wars, poltiics and the creation of legendary items.
